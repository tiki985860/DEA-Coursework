FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS (FAQs)

What time does check-in open?

+ -

The phoenix airlines check-in opens 3 hours before any scheduled departure.

when is the latest I can arrive at the gate before depature?

+ -

Boarding gates close 20 minutes before flight departure. If you arrive late, you will not be accepted onto the flight, your baggage will be offloaded and we won't offer a refund.

What happen if I'am denied travel?

+ -

If you're denied boarding on a flight for which you have a valid booking confirmation, you'll be entitled to travel on the next available flydubai flight. You'll also get refreshments and a flydubai voucher refunding the total amount of your booking. This does not apply if we refuse to carry you for reasons permitted by our Conditions of Carriage.

Can I fly during pregnanacy?

+ -

You can fly with flydubai as normal up to the end of your 28th week of pregnancy. If you decide to fly after the 28th week of pregnancy, you'll need to provide a medical certificate from your doctor or midwife. Please check our Flying while pregnant page for more information.

Can I prepurchace extra baggage allowance on the code share flights?

+ -

No, you cannot pre-purchase extra baggage allowance online on a codeshare flight. However, you can pay for excess baggage at the airport, at the operating carrier’s departing terminal.

What a age a new born child can be carried with phoenix airline?

+ -

You can travel with a new born child (infant) once they are 7 days old. Whether you're travelling with phoenix airlines, the age regulation is the same.

Which airline whould I need to contact to make changes to my flight booking?

+ -

If you have purchased your tickets from phoenix flights, you should contact Emirates if you have any queries related to your booking. Likewise, if you’ve purchased your ticket from flydubai, you should contact flydubai. However, both airlines will look after each other’s passengers throughout the journey especially when operational changes are required. If you’ve purchased your tickets from a travel agent, please contact your travel agent for changes.

If there is an unexpected delay or distruption who should I contact or approch or held?

+ -

phoenix airlines will be made aware of any delays and disruptions. The airline you are flying with will keep you informed of the situation and help with rebooking your flight if necessary. However, if you still want to get in touch with us, you can contact flydubai Customer Centre

Will bus transport provide between terminals?

+ -

There will be an airside inter-terminal transfer bus that will depart from Terminal 2 (T2) into Concourse A, B and C, in Terminal 3 (T3). The bus departs every 8 minutes and the one-way journey from T2 Concourse area to T3 (and vice versa) is about 35 minutes. This bus will be used for all classes of travel (Business and Economy Class).

Will I be through checked to my final destination?

+ -

Yes, if you’re travelling on an itinerary with phoenix services, you will be through-checked to your final destination. You will also be issued boarding passes for all sectors of your journey at the airport of departure.
